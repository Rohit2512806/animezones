Wealth and Wonder

Chu Yi, heir to a wealthy gaming dynasty, dives into the hyper-realistic VR game "Epoch" after his game-developer parents mysteriously disappear. Backed by a powerful AI, Kaos, and backed by a 30 billion-yuan bankroll, he levels up beyond everyone's expectations—posing as a weak player while secretly becoming a powerhouse. Soon he uncovers the Fantasy World Organization's dark conspiracies behind his parents’ vanishings. As “Mighty Whale” Chu Yi, he wages a secret war to save both the virtual world and his real life.

Genre: Sci‑Fi, Game Fantasy, Action, Comedy, Thriller

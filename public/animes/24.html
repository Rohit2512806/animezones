Martial Master

Qin Chen, once a legendary martial genius, is betrayed and cast into the Death Canyon. There, he awakens a mysterious ancient sword that grants him astonishing power. Fast forward 300 years: a new Qin Chen is born on Tianwu mainland, inheriting his predecessor’s legacy. As he uncovers conspiracies, cultivates his strength, and battles formidable foes, the cycle of revenge, redemption, and resurgence unfolds

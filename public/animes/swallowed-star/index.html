Swallowed Star

Season 6, also known as the Divine Realm Arc, follows Qin Yu and his sworn brothers as they ascend to the God Plane. Qin Yu learns divine laws, climbs the ranks, and aims to become a powerful Forging God to marry Jiang Li, who hails from one of the eight great divine families. To win her hand, he must present three unparalleled gifts—including a unique Hongmeng spiritual treasure. As they battle formidable enemies and navigate divine court politics, Qin Yu's journey escalates into a cosmic-scale saga. In a future Earth ravaged by monsters from space, humanity survives in fortified cities. The story follows Luo Feng, a young man who starts out as a poor delivery boy but rises through the ranks of martial arts and psychic cultivation to become one of Earth's strongest warriors. As he battles beasts, rival families, and intergalactic threats, Luo Feng learns shocking secrets about the universe, his family, and his true cosmic destiny. The show blends sci-fi with cultivation—from urban monster-slaying to space travel, AI, and ancient galactic empires.

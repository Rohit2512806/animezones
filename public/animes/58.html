The Divine Emperor of Destiny

The main character is Xuan Qi, an ordinary cultivator with little talent. He isn’t ranked among powerful divine-ability users and just wants a quiet life. Donghua World +1 One day, he accidentally gets caught in a fierce battle between a powerful Empress and an ancient demon. Through the chaos, Xuan Qi awakens a mysterious “system” (the “Heavenly Show-off / Tianxiu system”). This system grants him the ability to gain “Admiration Points” — basically, each time he carries himself like an unrivaled master, he earns power. Donghua World +2 DonghuaStream +2 As a result of that power, he ends up being mistaken for the “reincarnation of the Divine Emperor.” He suddenly gains miraculous strength: effortlessly defeats powerful demons and enemies of the demonic path, attracting attention, respect — even reverence — across the “Divine Province / Great Divine Land.

Genre: Xianxia, Adventure, Action, Martial Arts

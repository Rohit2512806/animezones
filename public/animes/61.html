The Record of Tang Dynasty

Set during the prosperous Tang Dynasty, the story follows investigators and officials who handle strange cases, hidden conspiracies, supernatural rumors, and political intrigue behind the glory of the empire. While the Tang Dynasty appears powerful and peaceful on the surface, dark secrets, mysterious crimes, cults, and hidden forces threaten the stability of the realm. Each case uncovers deeper truths about power, loyalty, and human nature. It blends historical realism with mystery and light supernatural elements, focusing on intelligence, deduction, and strategy rather than pure cultivation

Genre: Drama, Historical, Mystery, Adventure, Supernatural

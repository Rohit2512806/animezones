A Record of a Mortal’s Journey to Immortality – Season 2

The series follows Han Li, a humble and unremarkable young man born into a poor family. Despite his lack of talent, he enters the path of cultivation and immortality. Through perseverance, intelligence, and aid to others, he overcomes poison, joins minor sects, confronts demons and celestial beings, and steadily rises in power

Genre: Xianxia, Cultivation fantasy, Action, Supernatural

Release: 2021–2022

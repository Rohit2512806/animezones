Immortal Pet Fiercely Adorable

Set a century after the epic Battle of Shen Land, the world has regained peace at the cost of many lives. Tianquan, a powerful fairy whose soul is fractured and memory lost, now protects the mortal realm. She adopts a mysterious black bird pet, Lixiao, to keep her company. But tranquility shatters as inexplicable murders hint at a demonic resurgence. Unexpectedly, Lixiao transforms into human form, unveiling shocking ties between Tianquan’s missing memories, her lost lover Shao Weili, Lixiao’s new identity, and the defeated Demon King Shen

Genre: Fantasy, Romance, Adventure, Supernatural

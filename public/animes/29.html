My Senior Brother Is Too Steady – Season 2

The series follows Li Changshou, a terminally ill man reincarnated in ancient times. Armed with low-key cultivation tactics and strategies, he navigates the chaotic world of Qi refinement before the Conferred God War. In S2, he continues hiding his immense power, refines new spiritual techniques, and takes in a junior sister—while slyly playing a strategic game in sect politics

Genre: Cultivation, Comedy, Action, Adventure

Release: Dec 14, 2023

One Hundred Thousand Years of Qi Refining

An epic cultivation saga following Xu Yang, a disciple of the ancient Tianlan Sect. After refining Qi for tens of thousands of years in solitude, he emerges to find his sect nearly extinct. Driven by loyalty and power, he vows to resurrect Tianlan Sect, endure cosmic mysteries, and face divine forces, forging his legacy across realms

Genre: Cultivation, Fantasy, Action, SuperPower

Release: February 18, 2023 on Tencent (web series)

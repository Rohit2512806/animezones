Throne of Seal

Throne of Seal (Sealed Divine Throne)\n\"Throne of Seal\" is a Chinese animated series (donghua) based on the popular web novel \"Sealed Divine Throne\" written by Tang Jia San Shao, one of China's most famous fantasy authors.\n\nIn a world plagued by powerful demon armies, humanity is on the brink of extinction. To fight back against the darkness, elite warriors known as Templar Knights were formed to protect the last strongholds of mankind.\n\nThe story follows a young boy named Long Hao Chen, who dreams of becoming a Templar Knight. Driven by determination, talent, and a noble heart, he embarks on a journey filled with trials, dangerous missions, and ancient secrets. As he grows stronger, he discovers that his destiny is tied to the legendary Throne of Seal, a divine power that could turn the tide of war between humans and demons.

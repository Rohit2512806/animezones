The Angle next door spoils me rotten

The series follows Amane Fujimiya, a socially awkward high schooler who lives alone in a messy apartment. His neighbor is the school's most popular girl, Mahiru Shiina, known as an "angel" for her beauty and perfect grades. Their relationship starts when Amane lends her an umbrella on a rainy day; out of gratitude, Mahiru begins to look after him by cooking and cleaning, leading to a slow-burn, wholesome romance. Following the sweet confession at the end of Season 1, the new season will dive deeper into Mahiru and Amane's lives as an official couple. Expect to see more school events, personal growth, and Mahiru's continued "spoiling" of Amane.

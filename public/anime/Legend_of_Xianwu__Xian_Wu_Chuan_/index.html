Legend of Xianwu (Xian Wu Chuan)

Set in a fantastical realm filled with gods, demons, immortals, and Buddhas, we follow Ye Chen, a discarded and underestimated apprentice. Cast out from his sect, his fortunes change when he acquires a True Fire, setting him on a path of cultivation and martial arts mastery. As Ye Chen journeys through tumultuous realms, he battles otherworldly adversaries—and begins to forge his destiny as a revered immortal warrior

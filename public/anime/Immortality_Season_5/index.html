Immortality Season 5

Immortality (Chinese: 永生, Yong Sheng) is a donghua adapted from the fantasy web novel of the same name by Meng Ru Shen Ji. It follows Fang Han, a boy born into servitude who rejects being a slave and embarks on a journey of cultivation, struggles, and self-determination as he strives to become stronger and achieve true immortality through perseverance and mastery of Divine Arts. standardanime.com Seasons 1–4 have already been released, with the 3rd and 4th building on Fang Han’s journey and world-building
